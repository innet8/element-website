Why encrypted chat

The efficient and convenient communication mode of instant messaging platform has truly narrowed the space distance between people, and has become an indispensable communication tool for both individual users and enterprise users. However, in recent years, frequent information leakage incidents on the media also make some people put forward more and more questions about the security of communication software and user privacy protection.

What is end-to-end encryption

State-of-the-art end-to-end encryption (supported by open source signaling protocols) ensures the security of your conversations. We cannot read your messages or listen to your calls, nor can anyone else. Privacy is not an optional mode - it's just how Eim works. The encryption key exists on the user's device and nowhere else. As a result, no one - not even Eim - can read your message.

What is Snapchat

Messages will be automatically destroyed from both devices when the time is up. Timing method of private chat: after reading by the other party, both parties start timing and destroying at the same time; Group chat timing mode: Send/receive, message starts to time and destroy independently

What is a two-way retraction？

With two-way destruction, all chats are simultaneously destroyed from your primary and secondary devices, as well as from all devices of the other party. Group chat: Destroys all chat records on all devices of all users of the group.
